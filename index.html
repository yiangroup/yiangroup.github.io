关于我们

YIAN GROUP is a bilingual learning initiative focused on biostatistics and statistical programming. We are building a professional online space where learners, researchers, analysts, and working professionals can access practical training resources.

Our focus includes biostatistics, statistical programming, clinical research data, SAS, R, Python, CDISC, and applied data analysis.
